Based on Imaging Techniques

Holographic Imaging

  • Utilizes the phase information of terahertz waves
  • Reconstructs the three-dimensional shape and internal structure of the sample through computational

Tomographic Imaging

  • Utilizes terahertz radiation to penetrate the sample
  • Acquires projection data from different angles
  • Reconstructs the three-dimensional structure of the sample through computational methods

Ghost Imaging

  • Separates illumination and detection paths
  • Modulates terahertz waves using a spatial modulator
  • Reconstructs the image through computational methods

Near-field Imaging

  • Utilizes the near-field effects of terahertz waves
  • Enhances imaging resolution through computational methods
